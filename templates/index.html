Crop and Disease Identification

List of available crops for identification are

  • Apples
  • Grapes
  • Potatoes

List of available diseases for identification are

  • Apple Scab
  • Apple Rust
  • Grape Black Rot
  • Grape Black Measles
  • Potato Early Blight
  • Potato Late Blight

Instructions

When wanting crop identification
  • Upload an image where the crop is the main focus of the image by having the object in the centre
  • Only apples, grapes, and potatoes are identifiable, providing different crops will give incorrect results
When wanting disease identification
  • Upload an image of the diseased leaf, ideally having the leaf in the centre of the image
  • Disease identification will work only on listed diseases above, but might still give indication to other diseases, since some diseases tend to be similar
  • The model is also trained for healthy leaves

Info

This program was developed for a thesis study. It makes use of Convoluted Neural Networks to identify different crops and common diseases associated with them
